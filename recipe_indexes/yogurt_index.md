# Greek Yogurt / Dessert Index

### YG001 — Berry Protein Bowl
*~325 kcal | 50 g protein | 21 g carbs | 5 g fat*
- 8 oz Greek yogurt (low fat / triple zero)
- 1 scoop protein powder (25 g protein)
- 95 g frozen berries

### YG002 — Biscoff Greek Yogurt Dessert
*~325–350 kcal | ~40–45 g protein | carbs vary | 5–10 g fat*
- Greek yogurt + protein powder (25 g protein per scoop)
- Biscoff cookies (~5 cookies per serving)
- Optional: 0 cal brown sugar + vanilla extract

### YG003 — Raspberry Jam Cheesecake Yogurt
*~250–260 kcal | 30 g protein | 17 g carbs | 1–2 g fat*
- 6 oz Greek yogurt
- 1 scoop protein powder (25 g protein)
- Raspberry preserves (~20 g / 50 cal per serving)
