# Biscoff Coffee Greek Yogurt

Category: Breakfast / Snack

Tags: high-protein | sweet-treat | quick | meal-prep | dessert-inspired | coffee | oats | biscoff

Ingredients:
- 4 oz Fage 0% Greek yogurt
- 1 scoop vanilla protein powder
- 1 tsp unsweetened cocoa powder
- 1 tbsp brewed coffee
- 1 Biscoff cookie, crumbled
- 5 g whole-grain oats

Macros per serving:
Protein: 40 g | Carbs: 18 g | Fat: 2.75 g | Calories: 289 kcal

Instructions:
1. Mix Greek yogurt and protein powder until smooth.
2. Stir in coffee and cocoa powder until evenly blended.
3. Sprinkle crushed Biscoff cookie and oats on top.
4. Serve immediately or refrigerate briefly for flavors to meld.

Notes:
- Breakfast/snack-style, dessert-inspired with coffee and oats. Adjust cookie amount to reduce carbs if desired.
