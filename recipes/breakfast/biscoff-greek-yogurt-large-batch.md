# Biscoff Greek Yogurt (Large Batch)

Category: Breakfast / Snack

Tags: high-protein | balanced | quick | meal-prep | nuts-free | chocolate-free

Serving: full serving (standard breakfast/snack portion)

Ingredients:
- 8 oz Fage 0% Greek yogurt
- 3 Biscoff cookies, crumbled on top

Macros per serving:
Protein: 50 g | Carbs: 31 g | Fat: 4.5 g | Calories: 360 kcal

Instructions:
1. Mix Greek yogurt until smooth.
2. Crumble Biscoff cookies on top.
3. Serve immediately or refrigerate for later.

Notes:
- Variation of the original dessert-style Biscoff yogurt; see `recipes/treats/biscoff-greek-yogurt-dessert.md` for the dessert variant.
- Suitable for meal-prep; adjust cookie count if you want lower carbs.
