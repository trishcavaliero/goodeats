# Peanut Butter Greek Yogurt Bowl

Category: Breakfast

Tags: high-protein | balanced | quick | meal-prep | nuts | chocolate | dairy | optional-treat

Ingredients:
- 236 g Fage 0% Greek yogurt
- 1 scoop chocolate protein powder
- 20 g peanut butter powder
- 10 g crushed peanuts
- 10 g Hershey’s chocolate, chopped

Macros per serving:
Protein: 63 g | Carbs: 25 g | Fat: 12 g | Calories: 410–415 kcal

Instructions:
1. Mix yogurt and protein powder until smooth.
2. Stir in PB powder until evenly distributed.
3. Top with crushed peanuts and chopped chocolate.

Notes:
- Good for meal-prep and keeps well covered in the fridge for up to 3 days.
