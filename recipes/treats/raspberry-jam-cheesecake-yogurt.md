# Raspberry Jam Cheesecake Yogurt

Category: Treats / Desserts

Tags: treat | snack | dairy

Ingredients:
- 6 oz Greek yogurt
- 1 scoop protein powder (25 g protein)
- Raspberry preserves (~20 g / 50 cal per serving)

Macros per serving:
Protein: 30 g | Carbs: 17 g | Fat: 1–2 g | Calories: 250–260 kcal

Instructions:
1. Combine yogurt with protein powder and top with raspberry preserves.

Notes:
- Confirm preserves portion if you want a different standard.
